About Me

22 years of age, but old at heart. Currently finishing my final year at UCSD as a Mathematics major. My hobbies include reading philosphical and literary works as well engaging in activities which involve substantial physical effort (running, lifting, basketball, hiking, etc). I am still a novice at programming but at times can surprise myself with a sudden flash of competence. Also, I see improvement practically every week. With time I hope to reach a level of skill that will grant me freedom and independence. Such a state, at least from the present limited perspective, seems far off into to the distant and opaque future. Nonetheless, I remain hopeful that so long as I am persistent, disciplined, and most imprortantly, honest, all my efforts will not be in vain.
